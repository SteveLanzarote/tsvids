LEARNING OUTCOMES FOR SESSION 1, MODULE 5:

 

Learning Objectives for Session 1

Following engagement with this session, students will:

  • Reflect on their dominant skills and qualities
  • Understand the nature of such skills and qualities and how they can be employed within the role of the SNA
  • Become familiar with certain theoretical influences on a range of strategies to promote independence in the students they support
  • Recognise the nature of stress and how it manifests within students’ lives and their own
  • Understand how to regulate stress by employing certain stress-management skills

 

This session includes two learning blocks. The first block, presented by Phyllis Clegg, focuses on personal skills and qualities while the second block, presented by Dr. Stephen O’Neill, focuses on the issue of stress and how stress can be understood and managed.
